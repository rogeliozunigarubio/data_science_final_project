    Since 1994 Cocotours has grown to be one of the leading independent destination-management companies of the Dominican Republic. Based in Punta Cana with a permanent counter at Punta Cana airport, and with staff and vehicles around the country, we can quote for transportation and tours as well as hotel reservations.
